# Synthesis Document Template

> Referenced from SKILL.md Phase 5. Load when compiling the final document after debate completion.

```markdown
# Expert Arena: [Question]

> **Status:** [Consensus reached / Partial consensus / No consensus]
> **Date:** [date]
> **Experts:** [Names, comma-separated]
> **Decision Owner:** [human — experts recommend, human decides]

---

## Verdict

[Clear answer — what was concluded. If consensus — a single recommendation.
If partial — the main recommendation + caveats.]

---

## Trust Summary

Aggregate evidence from all experts' final positions using WLNK (weakest link):

| Position | Champion | Level | Trust (WLNK) | Evidence Count |
|----------|----------|-------|---------------|----------------|
| [Position A] | [Expert name] | [L0/L1/L2] | [min score] | [N for / M against] |
| [Position B] | [Expert name] | [L0/L1/L2] | [min score] | [N for / M against] |

**Assurance levels:**
- **L2 (Verified)** — position backed by empirical evidence with Trust >= 0.7
- **L1 (Reasoned)** — logically sound arguments, moderate evidence (0.4 <= Trust < 0.7)
- **L0 (Observation)** — opinion-based or weak evidence (Trust < 0.4)

---

## Evidence Catalog

All evidence cited during the debate, consolidated and de-duplicated:

| # | Evidence | Cited By | For Position | R | CL | Score |
|---|---------|----------|-------------|---|-----|-------|
| E1 | [title + source] | [expert] | [A/B] | [R] | [CL] | [score] |
| E2 | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... | ... |

**Contested evidence** (where experts disagreed on R or CL ratings):

| Evidence | [Expert 1] Rating | [Expert 2] Rating | Resolution |
|----------|-------------------|-------------------|------------|
| [title] | R:[x] CL:[y] | R:[a] CL:[b] | [which rating is more justified and why] |

---

## Debate Progression

[Chronologically: who started, who challenged whom, key moments.
Show DIRECT challenges between experts — this is the most valuable part:
"[Name X] wrote to [Name Y]: ..." → "[Y] responded: ..."
Show position shifts: "[Name] changed position after [Z]'s argument"
Highlight evidence challenges: "[Name] disputed [evidence] CL rating because..."]

---

## Key Arguments FOR the Chosen Position

| # | Argument | Raised By | Evidence | Score | Why Persuasive |
|---|---------|-----------|---------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | ... | ... | [E#] | [score] | ... |

## Arguments AGAINST (and why accepted as tolerable risks)

| # | Argument | Raised By | Evidence | Score | Why Tolerable |
|---|---------|-----------|---------|-------|--------------|
| 1 | ... | ... | [E#] | [score] | ... |

## Remaining Disagreements (if any)

[What could not be reconciled — with evidence from both sides]

---

## Recommendation

[Final recommendation with nuances. Not simply "do A" — but "do A because X, Y, Z,
while accounting for risk W, which [Expert] pointed out.
Trust level: [L0/L1/L2], overall confidence: [High/Medium/Low].]

## Trade-offs Accepted

- [What we give up by choosing this — with evidence reference]
```
